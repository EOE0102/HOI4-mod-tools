[h1]FAQs[/h1]
  - Q: It crashed when loading!
  - A: Clear all cashes under folder X:\Users\[YOURNAME]\Documents\Paradox Interactive\Hearts of Iron IV, then reinstall HOI4. 

  - Q: It lags when peace conference pops up!
  - A: the calculation resource is heavily influenced by the amount of states. 
	Recommand: install HOI on SSD, better CPU (important), bigger memory (mine is 32G), faster memory(mine is 2666MHZ)
	
  - Q: More Provinces for XX country?
  - A: Yes and No. HOI4 has a limit(between 19K and 22K) of displaying provinces. Game will CRASH TO DESKTOP when too many provinces are displayed.
